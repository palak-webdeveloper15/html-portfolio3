About Me

I’m basically a mix of creative chaos and big imagination. One moment I’m laughing at the weirdest scenarios with my friends, and the next moment I’m thinking up dramatic story ideas like a full cinematic universe in my head. My brain doesn’t really enjoy boring things — it prefers stories, jokes, random “what if this happened?” moments, and turning normal situations into something that feels like a movie scene. If there’s chaos, creativity, or a funny idea happening somewhere, there’s a good chance I’m involved.

At the same time, I like turning ideas into actual things. I enjoy thinking about films, editing, stories, and creative projects instead of just imagining them and forgetting about them. When I get an idea, I start wondering how it could actually work — what the scene would look like, what music would play, or how the story would unfold. That curiosity pushes me to explore new tools and learn new things so I can bring those ideas to life.

I also really enjoy the moments I spend with people around me. Whether it’s joking with friends, creating random chaotic scenarios, or just laughing at something completely unexpected, those moments make everything more fun. I like the feeling of turning an ordinary day into something memorable. In a way, I’m just someone who enjoys creativity, fun, and a little bit of chaos — because life is way more interesting when it’s not boring.
